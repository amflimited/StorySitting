Growth engine

Ten big moves now shaping StorySitting.

These are the product and market directions that make StorySitting more than a nice keepsake. The company starts with a moment, gathers family material, and moves people toward a finished Capsule.

01

Transition Story Tracks

Focused project paths for homes, moves, milestone birthdays, reunions, anniversaries, farms, family businesses, recipes, retirements, and goodbye seasons.

02

Free Story Map Front Door

The first win is a lightweight Story Map showing what the family has, what is missing, and which Capsule path makes sense.

03

Family Voice Collection

Relatives can submit voice by website recording, uploaded audio, call-in imports, guided interviews, or simple recorder kits when phones are a barrier.

04

Contributor Roles

Families are guided into useful roles: memory keepers, photo finders, question askers, recipe holders, voice contributors, place witnesses, and detail checkers.

05

Story Readiness Score

Each Story Room can show whether there are enough people, photos, voices, questions, and themes to move into a Signature Session.

06

Family Support Circle

Relatives who contribute memories can also help move the project from “we should” to “we finished it,” without one person carrying all the energy.

07

Story Day in a Box

Senior communities, churches, reunion hosts, and transition partners can host a Story Day while StorySitting handles intake, prompts, maps, and production.

08

Partner Story Layer Kits

Photographers, estate professionals, downsizing guides, and community leaders get a clear add-on: they keep the trust, StorySitting handles the story layer.

09

After-Capsule Upgrades

The first Capsule can unlock printed books, story cards, framed quotes, reunion slideshows, audio keepsakes, second interviews, sibling add-ons, and home chapters.

10

Sample Capsule Library

StorySitting will sell proof, not theory: sample Capsules for kitchen table stories, family homes, farms, recipes, anniversaries, retirements, and reunions.

Product shift

The product no longer starts with a package. It starts with the moment.

A family can begin with the person, place, object, recipe, gathering, move, or milestone they are afraid of losing. StorySitting turns that trigger into a Story Room, then a Story Map, then a finished Capsule path.
