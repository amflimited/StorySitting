Why this matters

The house gets cleaned out. The stories usually do too.

Families often save the furniture, boxes, photos, tools, recipe cards, and property papers. But the meaning behind them gets scattered because nobody has time to become the family interviewer, archivist, photo organizer, writer, and project manager.

StorySitting gives the family one private place to contribute, then turns the material into a guided interview and finished keepsake.
