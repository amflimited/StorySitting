StorySitting project terms.

StorySitting provides guided family story sessions, Story Rooms, Story Maps, and Story Capsule production support.

Project material

By submitting memories, photos, recordings, documents, or notes, you confirm that you have permission to share that material for the private family story project.

Deliverables

Deliverables vary by package and may include edited story sections, quotes, captions, selected audio excerpts, a private digital capsule, printable files, or related keepsake material.

Private family use

StorySitting projects are intended for private family use unless written permission is given for public examples or promotional use.
