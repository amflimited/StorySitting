Privacy

Your family’s story stays private by default.

StorySitting uses submitted memories, photos, recordings, documents, and interview material to prepare private family story projects.

We do not publish identifiable family material publicly without permission.

What we collect

Names, contact information, submitted memories, uploaded files, photos, voice notes, interview material, project notes, and information needed to prepare a Story Room, Story Map, session, or Story Capsule.

How we use it

We use project material to organize contributions, prepare questions, guide interviews, edit story material, produce Story Capsule deliverables, and communicate about the project.
