The real problem

Most families do not lack stories. They lack a way to finish them.

The memories are spread across siblings, parents, grandparents, phones, photo boxes, group texts, recipes, houses, and half-finished conversations. StorySitting gives everyone a simple way to contribute, then turns the scattered pieces into a guided interview and a finished keepsake.
