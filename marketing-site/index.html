The problem

Most families have stories everywhere, but no clear way to finish the project.

The memories are spread across relatives, phones, photo boxes, group texts, recipes, and old conversations. StorySitting gives everyone a simple way to contribute, then turns the scattered pieces into a guided interview plan and a finished keepsake.

Finished proof

A Story Capsule is the finished object families can read, hear, share, print, and keep.

It is not just a transcript, folder, or memory dump. It is an edited family story project shaped from the material your family contributes.
